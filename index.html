The difference between padding, margins and borders

Paddings The inner space between the content and the border of your box.
is used to create space around an element's content, inside of any defined borders.

MarginThe outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box.
Clears an area outside the border.

BordersThe perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line.
A border that goes around the padding and content.
